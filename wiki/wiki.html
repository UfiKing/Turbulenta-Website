Description

Jan Kontestabile is a medium sized money jew. He wears glasses for better vision since he does not want his coin to escape him. He errands his keep by teaching younglings about the art of communicating in foreign tongues, so they will be able to give him more coin for his collection.

He is of bland appearance to better fit in with the humanoid population.
