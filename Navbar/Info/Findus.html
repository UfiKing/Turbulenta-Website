Where to find us

The D&D game itself is happening on the discord server DigiMC, which is the offical discord server of Mladi zmaji. The game is running Amulet D20 which partnered with Mladi zmaji to bring us Turbulenta.

Amulet D20 also hosts an event every thursday in Medvode at 17:00, where almost 100 people gather to paint minifigures, play a variety of boardgames, play D&D and much more. The event is also free and we have a bring-take snacks system.
