Welcome to Turbulenta!

Turbulenta is a (free) online D&D campaign, that was started in 2023 by Mladi zmaji, in collaboration with Amulet D20. It's played on the official Mladi Zmaji discord server DigiMC.

Chaos Children Episode 1: How do you like your taters?

Caracters:
  • Ceres Liontari - Timmy
  • Caraxes - Daevala
  • Thian - ReallyHighWitch
  • Morwyn - Daevala
  • Minamoto No Tametomo - Tatriks

Caraxes looks up at the tall, helpless fingerling. He's just standing there, looking after where the mistress went. "I am herre.."

Ceres is entirely fascinated by what he sees. He is uncertain how to proceed though, for he has never seen a fiend before, at least not in person. "It is a pleasure to meet you, little mister," he begins, trying to be polite while doing a quick bow. "Hmm... Are you the master of Lady Morwyn and commander Minamoto? Are they your loyal servants? A proper title befits a creature such as yourself, but I must admit, I do not know how to address you. Will Sir Devil Baby suffice?" he asks in genuine curiosity.

Caraxes puffs himself up, finally someone to see how great he was. "I am Caraxes, once known as Lord Al'xitar, Holderrr of the Gate, Ssslayer of the Drreadhorrrrorrss and Tyrrrant of the Dawnfox Coassst. Fallen in the age of the Black rain. I have been ssserrrving in Hellssss for almost 2 millenniasss and I am contracted to ssserve Morwyndell Eldamar until herrr demissse." He proudly recites, his long line of achievements. Now he must only serve his contract before he ascends. " is mistrressess new mate. And ssshe hass invited you .... To live in herrr domain."
